# Welcome to Nebula

Nebula is a cutting-edge design system that blends retro-futuristic aesthetics with modern functionality. Drawing inspiration from the bold vision of 1970s space age design, the clean geometry of Bauhaus, and the innovative LCARS (Library Computer Access/Retrieval System) interface from Star Trek, Nebula offers a unique and cohesive visual language for your digital products.

## Key Features

- **Retrofuturistic Color Palette**: Vibrant hues reminiscent of 70s sci-fi, balanced with Bauhaus-inspired primaries
- **Modular Components**: Flexible, reusable UI elements that embody the efficiency of LCARS
- **Typography**: Clean, sans-serif fonts that echo both Bauhaus simplicity and futuristic clarity
- **Responsive Layouts**: Fluid grid systems that adapt seamlessly across devices
- **Accessibility**: (Coming soon) <strike>WCAG 2.1 compliant components ensure inclusive design</strike>
